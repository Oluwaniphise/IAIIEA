How to join

IAIIEA Membership

The membership is open to everyone who is involved in the business of testing, measurement, examinations and assessment irrespective of discipline, affiliation and faculty. This includes individuals or corporate bodies such as teachers, lecturers, professors, researchers, post graduate students, evaluators, item writers and developers, psychometricians, software developers, ICT experts, medical educators and other professionals associated with education.

Conditions for Membership

Members may not necessarily be experts in the field of Education but should be working for educational assessment and must be ready to take education to the next level of excellence. Benefits IAIIEA offers members opportunity to:
  • share experiences in educational assessment;
  • equip themselves with practical knowledge in psychometrics and other assessment software tools;
  • collaborate with member states, organizations and individuals in handling effective assessment matters;
  • develop and have access to innovative software for item analysis and calibrations;
  • access to publications, free weekly webinars and e-conference participation;
  • participate in capacity building programmes and workshops and
  • share new ideas on assessment amongst members.

Application for membership

Send a request to info@iaiiea.org or otherwise visit the website: www.iaiiea.org and send your request.
