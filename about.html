ABOUT us at IAIIEA

The International Association for Innovations in Educational Assessment is an Association for innovative assessment with the mission of promoting the science and practice of innovations in educational assessment. The association aims at:

  • Developing innovations in educational assessment in all levels;
  • Promoting educational assessment innovations for national and International cohesion and;
  • Providing intensive capacity building for researchers and students on latest technology and software for data analysis.

VISON

IAIIEA has its vision to be a pace-setter and a world-class association for innovative educational assessment.

MISSON

To advance innovative system that enhances quality assessment in terms of intellectual competence and the zeal to add value to our world.

MOTTO

Innovation for execellence
