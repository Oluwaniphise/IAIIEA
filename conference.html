THEME: "REDESIGNING EDUCATIONAL ASSESSMENT FOR 21ST CENTURY.

CALL FOR PAPERS

We seek your contributiongs to a journal on innovations in educational assessment and paper for oral presentation at the annual conference of the above name association. The sub-themes are as follows:

  • Affective ambivalence and students test aversion issues
  • Assessment and attainment
  • Assessment characterized by digital culture
  • Assessment methods in art, humanities, science, mathematics and technology
  • Pass/fail dichotomy in assessment
  • Demand for 21st century skills-critical thinking and creativity
  • e-assessment to e-learning
  • Engineering and assessment
  • Feedback and Feedforword in education
  • Inclusiveness in educational assessment
  • New areas in research
  • Investigating critical & contemporary issues in education high stakes assessment for national and international cohesion
  • Medical education and assessment
  • Modern reforms in test construction and validation
    • Modern test theory and assessment
    • Role of psychometrics in educational research and assessment
    • Score reporting methods
    • Using assessment to promote all learning
    • Utilizing adaptive testing in assessment
    • Gender equality and assessment
    • Disability inclusion in assessment
